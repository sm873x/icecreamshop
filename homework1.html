Our Story

Stella's Ice Cream Castle came into being not so long ago in a not too distant land.

It began as a simple weekend quest to find the perfect lychee flavored ice cream. Alas, lychee was not yet a popular flavor in America, so Stella decided to make her own. This is how Stella's ICC was born. In a small kitchen on Capitol Hill in Washington D.C.

We have 4 main flavors available all year round and an additional seasonal flavor.

Now let's ice cream!
